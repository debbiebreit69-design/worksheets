🔍 Reading – Read and Choose / קרא ובחר
קרא את הסיפור הקצר וענה על השאלות.
Dan is a soldier in the army. One day, his team got a call about a dangerous situation. A young boy was missing near the entrance of a forest. It was dark — there was a lot of darkness. The government asked the army to help with the rescue. Dan was worried, but he was also brave. He used his phone to contact the other soldiers. They searched in the nearby forest and did not stop. They wanted to succeed. Finally, they found the boy. He was safe! Dan realized that working together is the best way to help people.
Choose the correct answer. / בחר את התשובה הנכונה.
1. Who is Dan?
2. What was the problem?
3. How did Dan contact the soldiers?
4. Where did they search?
5. Was the boy safe at the end?
