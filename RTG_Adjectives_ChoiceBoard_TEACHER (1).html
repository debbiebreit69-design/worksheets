📖 Vocabulary – Opposites
אוצר מילים – הפכים

Read and choose the correct answer. / קרא ובחר.
1. In the morning, the market is...
2. At night, the market is...
3. The opposite of "noisy" is...
4. The food lines in the morning are long. At night they are...
